☆ Mattys Style Guide ☆


As stated previously in the "About Me" tab, styling feels like a fun mission for me to complete. I would definitely say my style has elevated over the years, now prioritizing curated layering, and intentional silhouettes.


The "Sleazy" Influence

As my personal style continues to evolve, I realize how much my tastes have matured, but maintained that raw, unapologetic edge.

I've become very into the "Sprezzatura" vibe, aka "Calculated Effortlessness"


Heavy Layering

The key rule for me is when in doubt, throw on a cardigan, jacket, vest, etc. This enhances the relaxed look, and overall silhouette of the outfit.

Rule: If you feel something is missing, LAYER!

Industrial accents

I've been super into statement pieces; Long necklaces, chunky bracelets, macrame Belts, list goes on. Many people tend to forget how much accessories elevate your look.

Rule: Don't EVER be afraid to "over-accessorize"

Shoe Choice

I've started to deviate away from always wearing sneakers (lowkey because my jeans are too long) but also because I've found that heels, wedges, boots, just look more intentional, and resonates more with the person I'm becoming.

Rule: A casual heel NEVER hurts!

Compliment instead of Match

When all you do is match, your outfits start feeling repetitive. Complimenting adds tension. Pairing pieces that speak the same language without saying the exact words.

Rule: Style the accents, instead of the base

Shop Secondhand

Shopping secondhand isn’t only eco-friendly; it’s like a real jackpot for your wardrobe, and your wallet too. True vintage clothes usually come with sturdy fabrics, strong stitching, and those deep denim shades that today’s fast fashion can’t really mimic. And all of that for way less than retail prices.

Rule: Shop for QUALITY!

Dress for yourself

Most importantly, dress how YOU see fit. Stop caring about unwanted opinions, they aren't serving you anything but misery. Having an authentic style should come with confidence!

Rule: Stop caring
